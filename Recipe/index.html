Gulam Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 5 to 6 tablespoons milk
  • 1 cup full-fat milk powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking powder

Recipe

  1. Prepare the dough.
  2. Prepare the sugar syrup
  3. Roll the jamuns
  4. Fry the jamuns
  5. Add jamuns to the sugar syrup

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • 2 cups besan
  • Desi ghee
  • 1 tablespoon magaz

Recipe

  1. Take desi ghee in a heavy kadai and let it melt.
  2. Once the ghee has melted, then add cups of besan in parts.
  3. Mix very well and continue to stir the besan non stop on a low heat.
  4. The ghee will be absorbed by the besan and you will see the mixture getting thicker.
  5. Once the besan has roasted well keep the pan down.
  6. Preparing sugar syrup
  7. Add the sugar syrup in the besan mixture and mix it well.
